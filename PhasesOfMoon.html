The Moon is between Earth and the Sun. We see the dark side.

Why do phases happen?

Sunlight Exactly half of the moon is always lit by the sun, just like Earth.
Orbit As the moon orbits Earth, we see different amounts of that lit half.
Perspective The "phases" are just our changing view of the moon's day and night sides.
